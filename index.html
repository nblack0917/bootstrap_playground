Be safe a night

Make sure to put away any food items or containers before going to bed. Many animals are on the lookout for a cooler or bag of chips. They can ruin your breakfast without even waking you up.

Breakfast recipes

Just Desserts!

One of my favorite things about camping in making fun desserts in the camp fire.

There are many different things you can make for dessert.

Dessert is the best meal!

Banana Smores

Peel banana and slice lengthways. Place on sheet of foil and bend sides of foil up to make a boat shape. Fill center of banana with mini marshmallows. Sprinkle chocolate chips over marshmallows. Close top and sides of foil tightly. Cook over hot coals for 8-10 mintues. Open foil to cool for several minutes. Enjoy with a spoon.

Baked Hot Cinnamon Apple

Core apple. A sweet variety of apple is preferred. Place on sheet of foil. Fill core with Red Hot© candies and top with 1 tablespoon of butter. Wrap foil tightly around apple. Place in coals for 10-12 minutes. Open foil and let cool several minutes. Enjoy.

Baileys Dipped Marshmallows (21+)

Pour seveal ounces of Baileys Irish Cream© liqueur into a short cup or bowl. Skewer and roast standard size marshmallow over camp fire to your liking. Quickly dip marshmallow into liqueur to coat. Eat while still hot. Good stuff.

Fire Roasted Starburst

Skewer Startburst© candy and roast over camp fire for 1-2 minutes. Eat. Enough said.
